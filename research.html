OUR COLLECTION

The Osgoode Township Museum has over 10,000 artefacts in its holdings ranging from original land deeds to farm machinery. Explore our exciting artifact collection here dedicated to preserving the history of agricultural and rural heritage in the Osgoode Township. The Museum’s artefact records are available online thanks to a joint initiative between the Ottawa Museum Network and the City of Ottawa, including the Museums and Heritage Programs Unit and the City of Ottawa Archives. Presently, Osgoode Township Museum has a limited number of records online but staff and volunteers are working diligently to expand our online database. From the Collection
